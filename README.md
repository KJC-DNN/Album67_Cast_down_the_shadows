# Album 67 - Cast down the shadows

Album 67 is a high-intensity worship/prophetic project blending hard rock, orchestral elements, and choir-driven anthems. It frames spiritual warfare imagery—matrices, false gods, digital codes—into songs calling for Christ’s truth to triumph over oppression, deception, and cultural idols. Use with pastoral caution: emphasize repentance, justice, healing, and nonviolence.

### Prayer

Sovereign Lord, we name the powers that oppress and call on Your truth to prevail. Keep our fight humble and merciful, direct us to pray and to act with justice, and guard our hearts from hatred. May these songs point people to Christ, the true Redeemer, and send us forth as peacemakers and servants of Your kingdom. Amen.
